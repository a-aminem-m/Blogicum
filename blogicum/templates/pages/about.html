About the project

The Blogicum is a home for creative people. This is a community of people for whom there is no line between blogging and friendship on social networks.

Friendship and stories about new, unexplored experiences — that's it what you will find on our resource. There are millions of blogs on various topics. Travel, politics, entertainment, fashion, literature, design and everything other spheres of human activity.

Creativity, diversity and freedom of opinion and expression — the main features of our users.
